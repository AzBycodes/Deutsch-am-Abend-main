Why Deutsch am Abend

A non-profit built by volunteers to support community learners.

Mission-driven

We exist to make quality German education accessible to Filipino learners. No shareholders — just a community reinvesting in itself.

Small groups

We design our schedules around your busy life. Enjoy small, engaging class sizes where your goals get the dedicated attention they deserve.

Outcomes-focused

Every level maps to CEFR standards. Progress is measurable and recognised internationally, opening doors to study and work.

How it works

Your pathway from A1 to B2 — or exam ready.

Start at the level that matches where you are. Every course is structured around CEFR frameworks, so progression is clear and internationally recognized. Whether you're aiming for a placement abroad, further study, or just fluency — we build the path with you.

What sets us apart is our commitment to our teachers. We continually invest in their growth and development because exceptional education begins with exceptional educators. Teacher development isn't a perk — it's core to our mission.
