Discounted Pricing

Who can get the discounted price?

SPFL-PASCH Program

You qualify for the discounted course fee automatically if you are:

  • an alumnus/alumna of the SPFL-PASCH program, or
  • a current SPFL-PASCH German student

Not in SPFL-PASCH?

You can still receive the discounted registration fee by becoming a Deutsch am Abend Community Member — see details below.

Community Membership · Valid for 2 years

Become a Community Member!

If you're not eligible for the SPFL-PASCH discount, you can unlock the same discounted course pricing by joining as a Deutsch am Abend Community Member.

  • Discounted registration fees on every course
  • Access to intercultural community events
  • Online Study Lounge / Tutorial sessions (1–2x per week)
  • Membership is valid for 2 years from signing
How to apply:
  1. Download the membership application form (PDF) below.
  2. Fill it in and sign it.
  3. Pay the membership fee — ₱500 (students) or ₱1,000 (non-students) — as instructed on the form.
  4. Email the signed form together with your proof of payment to info.deutschamabend@gmail.com.
  5. We'll confirm your membership and apply your discounted course pricing within 2–3 business days.
Download Membership Form (PDF)

Remarks: If you are not eligible for the SPFL-PASCH discount, purchasing Community Membership is usually worthwhile because the registration discount is greater than the membership cost.
