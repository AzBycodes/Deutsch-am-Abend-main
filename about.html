About Us

Deutsch am Abend: Empowering a community of German learners in the Philippines.

We believe quality language education should be accessible to everyone, taught by committed instructors, and reinvested into growth — not extracted as corporate profit.

Our story

Started by volunteers,
run by a community.

Deutsch am Abend began as evening classes among a small group of passionate German-language educators and learners in the Philippines. We saw a gap: quality German instruction was hard to find, prices were high, and the focus was always on volume, not on learner outcomes or teacher wellbeing.

As a mission-driven non-profit, our financial framework is designed to maximize educational impact. We continually reinvest in our teachers' growth and development because exceptional education begins with exceptional educators—reflecting a permanent organizational commitment to instructional excellence.

How we operate

Transparency and mission-alignment in everything.

No shareholders

We're non-profit. Revenue from tuition covers teacher pay, learning materials, venues, and operational costs. Any surplus is reserved for the Teacher Training Scholarship Fund.

CEFR-aligned

All courses map to international standards (A1–B2). Your certificate of completion is recognized globally, opening pathways to work and study abroad.

Community-first pricing

We keep tuition accessible and transparent. Discount arrangements available for groups and students — ask us.

Help us grow 🌱 — donate today!

Unlike for-profit language schools, we don't have investors. Donations from individuals and organizations directly fund accessible tuition, learning materials, and our teacher training scholarships.
